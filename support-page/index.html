Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the platform?

Explore DSA topics, quizzes, practice problems, and learning roadmaps from the homepage. Start with the Topics section to begin learning.

How do I report a bug?

Use the bug report section above and explain the issue clearly. Include steps to reproduce and screenshots if possible.

How can I track my progress?

Your progress is automatically tracked in the Dashboard. You can view XP, completed topics, and earned badges.

How can I contact support?

Use the support request form above or create a GitHub issue. We'll respond within 24-48 hours.

Is this platform free?

Yes! Algo Infinity Verse is completely free and open-source. We're committed to making DSA learning accessible to everyone.
