Fractional Indexing CRDT (Logoot / LSEQ)

In collaborative editors (like Google Docs), assigning integer indices (0, 1, 2) breaks down during concurrent edits. This engine assigns Fractional Strings to characters to maintain absolute total ordering across the network without central locking.
