🎯 Interview Tips

❓ Always Clarify First

Never jump to designing. Ask about scale, read/write ratio, latency requirements, and whether you need strong or eventual consistency.

📊 Estimate Before Designing

Back-of-envelope calculations show you understand scale. Estimate QPS, storage, bandwidth — then let it guide your architecture choices.

🏛️ Start High Level

Draw the big picture first: clients, load balancer, services, database, cache. Then drill into the hardest components.

🗃️ Justify DB Choices

Don't just say "use MySQL". Explain why: relational for transactions, NoSQL for unstructured data, time-series for metrics.

💾 Always Mention Caching

Where to cache, what to cache, what eviction policy (LRU/LFU), and what consistency problems caching introduces.

⚖️ Discuss Trade-offs

Interviewers want to hear you reason about trade-offs — consistency vs availability, cost vs latency, SQL vs NoSQL.
