Complexity Notes

Doubling Time

O(n log n)

Doubling Space

O(n)

The doubling method sorts suffixes by a pair of ranks: (rank[i], rank[i + 2^k]). Each round doubles the compared prefix length, until all suffixes become uniquely ranked.
