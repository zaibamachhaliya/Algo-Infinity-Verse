🔥 Why Dynamic Replanning Matters

🤖

Mars Rover Navigation

NASA's Mars rovers use D*Lite — a dynamic replanning algorithm — because terrain data from sensors updates constantly. A rock that wasn't visible from orbit becomes an obstacle mid-path. The rover must replan without starting over from scratch.

🎮

Game AI Pathfinding

In RTS games, thousands of units pathfind simultaneously. When a unit blocks a path, all affected units must replan. Full A* restarts are too expensive — games use hierarchical pathfinding and flow fields to handle dynamic obstacles efficiently.

🗺️

GPS Rerouting

When Google Maps detects a traffic jam on your route, it triggers a replan. The key insight: only the portion of the path affected by the new obstacle needs to be recomputed — not the entire route from your current position.

📊

A* vs BFS Replanning

A* replans more efficiently because its heuristic guides it toward the target — it explores fewer cells. BFS must explore all equidistant cells regardless of direction. After an obstacle fires, watch how A* finds a new path with far fewer visited cells than BFS.
