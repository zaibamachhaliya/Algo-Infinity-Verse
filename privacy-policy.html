Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Introduction

Algo Infinity Verse is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information (Google Sign-In via Firebase Authentication)
  • User profile details (name, avatar, preferences)
  • Quiz performance and progress data
  • Practice activity and usage behavior
  • Feedback or support messages

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide personalized learning experience
  • To save progress, XP, and achievements

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using Firebase Firestore. Authentication is handled through Firebase Authentication.

We use security rules and best practices to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Authentication (Sign-In)
  • Firebase Firestore Database

  • Analytics tools (if enabled in future)

6. Your Rights

  • Request correction or deletion of data
  • Withdraw consent (by deleting account)
  • Contact us for any privacy concerns

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
